# NeoSwag — Coming Soon Page Brief

## Build This With Claude Code

Single page, mobile-first, cryptic teaser site. Should feel like discovering a signal from the future — like intercepted data from 2060.

---

## Tech

- Static HTML/CSS/JS (no framework needed, keep it simple)
- Mobile-first, responsive
- Host-ready (Vercel, Netlify, whatever)

---

## Vibe

**Cryptic. Not corporate "coming soon." More like a transmission you weren't supposed to find.**

Think: corrupted archive footage, recovered data, a signal bleeding through from another time. Dark, glowing, alive. The page itself should feel like it's barely holding together — like the content is trying to come through but the signal keeps breaking.

---

## Layout (Top to Bottom, Mobile)

### 1. Entry State

- Page loads to black/near-black
- A subtle static/grain texture covers everything (CSS noise or canvas grain)
- After a beat (~1-2 seconds), content starts fading/glitching in

### 2. The Word

- **"NEOSWAG"** — large, bold, centered
- Should feel like it's being transmitted, not just displayed
- Glitch effect on the text — occasional flicker, horizontal displacement, RGB split
- Font: something heavy and wide. Try **Monument Extended**, **Tusker Grotesk**, or **Bebas Neue** as a fallback. Load from Google Fonts or self-host.

### 3. The Line

- Below the title, one line of text fades in after a delay:
- **"MONEY IS TEMPORARY. SWAG IS ETERNAL."**
- Smaller, tracked out, uppercase
- Subtle glow — like neon behind frosted glass
- Could pulse very slowly (opacity or text-shadow)

### 4. The Signal

- Some kind of ambient animation in the background or midground
- Ideas (pick one or combine):
  - Slow-moving gradient mesh (magenta → cyan → yellow, the NeoSwag palette)
  - Floating pixel particles
  - A single horizontal scan line slowly moving down the page on loop
  - Subtle ASCII characters drifting in the background
- Should feel alive but not distracting — atmospheric, not a screensaver

### 5. The Date (Optional / Cryptic)

- Don't say "Coming Summer 2026"
- Instead something like:
  - `2026.XX.XX` (redacted)
  - `SIGNAL INCOMING`
  - `TRANSMISSION PENDING`
  - `▓▓▓▓░░░░░░ 41%` (a fake loading bar that never finishes)
- Small, monospace font, near the bottom

### 6. Footer / Contact

- Tiny, bottom of page
- Just: `@adamfadi333` (linked to Instagram)
- Maybe a small envelope icon or email link
- Nothing else. No nav, no menu, no links.

---

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#0a0a0f` (near-black with a hint of blue)
- **Primary text**: `#f0f0f0` (off-white)
- **Magenta/Pink**: `#e930ff`
- **Cyan**: `#00e5ff`
- **Yellow**: `#ffea00`
- **Dim text**: `#666666`

The magenta/cyan/yellow should appear in glows, gradients, and accents — not as flat UI color. Think light bleeding through cracks.

---

## Typography

- **Title**: Something ultra-bold, wide, impactful. Monument Extended or similar.
- **Tagline**: Tracked-out uppercase, lighter weight. Could be the same family or a clean sans.
- **System text** (date, footer): Monospace. `JetBrains Mono`, `IBM Plex Mono`, or `Space Mono` from Google Fonts.

---

## Interactions

- **Hover on NEOSWAG** (desktop): intensify the glitch, RGB split widens
- **Tap anywhere** (mobile): brief glitch flash across the whole page
- **Scroll**: there's nothing to scroll to. The page is one viewport. Lock it or just don't add content below the fold.

---

## Things to AVOID

- Any "coming soon" template energy
- Email signup forms
- Countdown timers
- Stock gradients or generic blur backgrounds
- Anything that feels like a SaaS landing page
- Social media icon rows

---

## Reference Aesthetic

- Album teaser pages (Kanye's early DONDA rollout energy)
- Glitch art / datamosh culture
- Y2K web revival but darker
- The feeling of tuning into a pirate radio frequency at 3am

One screen. Black. NEOSWAG glitches into existence. "Money is temporary. Swag is eternal." glows underneath. Something alive moves in the background. A cryptic date fragment sits near the bottom. @adamfadi333 at the very bottom. That's it. Ship it.
